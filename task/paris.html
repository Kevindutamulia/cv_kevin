Paris

Paris is the capital of France and one of the most famous cities in the world, known for its rich history, art, fashion, and culture. With a population of over 2 million people, it is a major economic and cultural hub in Europe.

Located along the Seine River, Paris is home to iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The city is also renowned for its charming streets, world-class cuisine, and vibrant atmosphere, making it a top destination for tourists from around the globe.
