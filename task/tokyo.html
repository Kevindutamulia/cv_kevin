Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the most populous city in the world, with a metropolitan area of over 37 million people. Located on Honshu Island, Tokyo was originally known as Edo before becoming the center of government in 1868. The city is a hub of economy, culture, and technology, blending tradition with modernity.

Tokyo is famous for its landmarks such as Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, and the Imperial Palace, as well as being a global center for innovation and trends.
